Datasets generated and shared by the "High and low" U19 group are deposited on open community archives, grouped below by hosting solution. See the Data Science Core for the tools used to convert, process, and deposit these data.

Embargoed datasets are not yet publicly released. Their cards link to the draft page, accessible only to authorized users. An additional In press badge marks a dataset that will be released upon publication.
